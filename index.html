Projects

Toss Juggling In-Sim (and on Hardware)

Fall 2023

For my mechanics of manipulation course, I developed a simulation of a toss juggling robot in MuJoCo. I used reinforcement learning to learn a policy for the robot to juggle balls using DDPG. I also applied the co-design method to design a hardware system to embody the policy.

Model Predictive Control for Constrained Dynaamic Systems

Fall 2023
